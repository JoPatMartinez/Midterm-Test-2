Welcome to the Action Movie Blog!

Here we talk about some movies with the best action scenes, making you want to jump out of your seat and join in on the fun!

Transformers: Dark of the Moon

"In any war, there are calms between the storms. There will be days when we lose faith, days when our allies turn against us. But the day will never come, that we forsake this planet and its people."

After discovering a war-ridden and abandoned ship on the moon, Optimus Prime and the Autobots find out that their quarrel with the Decepticons is bigger than expected. The action scenes in this movie would want you to jump out of your seat and join Optimus Prime as you take back the city of Chicago from the Decepticons! Each fight scene, mixed in with the amazing music and audio, gets you pumped up and motivated to join in help claim victory for the Autobots!

Saving Private Ryan

"He better be worth it. He better go home and cure a disease, or invent a longer-lasting light bulb."

Captain John Miller is tasked to lead a group of soldiers across enemy lines to retrieve Private James Ryan in order to bring him home after the rest of his brothers were killed in battle. The action scenes here are realistic and hold no boundaries when it comes to the horrors of war. The infamous D-Day scene in this movie is considered by veterans to be the closest movie reenactment of the actual D-Day.

Predators

"“This planet is a game preserve. And we're the game. In case you didn't notice, we just got flushed out. They sent the dogs in, just like you if you were stalking boar or shooting quail. They split us apart and they watched. Testing us.”"

Awoken from falling and landing in a strange jungle, former black-ops soldier Royce and a group of trained mercenaries traverse the jungle trying to find reason as to who put them there, and why. As they try to find answers, they realize they're being watched and hunted. But not by a human. The action scenes here excite Predator fans alike, with famous moves and technologies from past movies being shown, more gore, and even some Predator vs. Predator action! These scenes will make you jump out of your seat!
